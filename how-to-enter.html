How to Enter the Raffle

Simple steps to your chance at winning epic Pokémon sealed products and single cards!

Your Journey To Pulling The Best Prize

We've designed our entry system to be as straightforward and transparent as possible. We run everything through our trusted, heavily populated Facebook community so you always know exactly who we are, who is winning, and how the draws happen in real-time. Follow the steps below to secure your entry into the next massive Pokémon giveaway!

1

Join The Community

Head over to our Facebook page and hit the follow button. This is your first step into a community of avid Pokémon TCG collectors. By following us, you'll be instantly notified the moment a new rare item, such as a First Edition Base Set pack or an Evolving Skies Elite Trainer Box, goes live on the platform.

2

Pick Your Prize & Numbers

Browse our active raffle posts on Facebook or via the Live Auctions page here on the site. Found something you like? Check the list of available numbers (e.g., 1 to 50), and comment on the post with the number you wish to purchase.

3

Secure Your Entry

Once your numbers are confirmed by our admins, you will receive a secure payment link. We only accept secure payment methods to protect your transaction. Once paid, your name is officially locked into the roster for those numbers.

4

Watch The Live Draw!

As soon as all spots fill up, we schedule a live stream on our Facebook page. We use Google's True Random Number Generator on screen to randomly select the winning number. If it lands on your number, you win the prize! No gimmicks, just pure excitement.

Raffle Rules & Terms

  • You must be a follower of the Vivid Wins Facebook page.
  • Entrants must be 18+ years of age or possess explicit parental consent.
  • Skill-Based Entry: To comply with UK Prize Competition regulations, participants must correctly answer a skill-based question to enter any draw.
  • Free Postal Entry: To enter any draw for free, you may send your name, contact details, and the correct answer to the skill question via first-class post to our registered office (see Contact page) before the draw closes.
  • Numbers are distributed strictly on a first-come, first-served basis according to comment timestamps.
  • Payments must be cleared before the live draw initiates. Unpaid spots will be passed to backups.
  • Live draws will never be recorded beforehand—you will always see the draw happen live.
  • Winners must claim their prize within 7 days by direct messaging our admin team.
  • We ship anywhere in the UK securely and, for high-value items, via tracked and insured postage.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

New to Pokémon raffles? Here is everything you need to know about how we operate, how we source our products, and how you can join the fun.

Are the Pokémon cards and booster boxes authentic?
Absolutely. We have a zero-tolerance policy for fakes. All of our sealed products are sourced from official UK Pokémon distributors, and our high-end singles are authenticated and graded by Professional Sports Authenticator (PSA), Beckett (BGS), or CGC.
How do you ensure the random number generator is fair?
Excellent question. We do all of our draws completely live on video. You will see us refresh the Google Random Number Generator multiple times to prove it is live and active. There are no cuts or edits in our videos. Thousands of followers trust our transparency.
What happens if a raffle doesn't fill up?
In the highly unlikely event that a raffle doesn't fill up within the specified timeframe (usually 2 weeks), we will perform the draw for the people who have already entered, or we will refund all entrants 100% of their money. You are never at risk of losing your entry fee without a draw taking place.
How fast is shipping if I win?
We dispatch prizes within 48 hours of you claiming your win. All items are packaged with extreme care (bubble wrap, sturdy boxes, and top-loaders for singles) to ensure your Pokémon treasure arrives in pristine condition.
