How to Enter the Raffle

Simple steps to your chance at winning!

Entry Steps

1

Follow Us on Facebook

Head over to our Facebook page and hit the follow button to stay updated on all our raffles and announcements.

2

Check Current Raffles

Browse our active raffle posts to see what prizes are available and the entry requirements for each raffle.

3

Follow Entry Instructions

Each raffle has specific entry instructions posted on Facebook. Follow these carefully to ensure your entry counts.

4

Wait for Winner Announcement

Winners are announced on Facebook. Make sure you're following our page to get notified immediately!

Raffle Rules

  • All entrants must follow our Facebook page to be eligible
  • You must be 18+ years old or have parental consent
  • One entry per person per raffle (unless otherwise stated)
  • Winners must respond within 7 days to claim their prize
  • All entries are final and cannot be modified
  • Winners are selected fairly and transparently
  • By entering, you agree to our terms and conditions

Prize Details

Each raffle has different prizes ranging from Pokémon cards and collectibles to exclusive merchandise. Prize details are posted with each individual raffle on our Facebook page. Make sure to check what you could win before entering!
