How Our Pokémon Raffles Work

Every auction listed above is a live, legitimate chance to win premium Pokémon collections. When you find an item you are interested in—such as a booster box or a graded card—simply click "View Details & Enter".

You'll be directed securely to our verified Facebook community where you can claim your specific numbers for the draw. Once all spots are filled, we lock the draw and go LIVE on video! We use a mathematically secure true random number generator to pick the winner directly in front of the audience, ensuring absolute peak transparency.

Tip: The "Almost Full" raffles are the ones that will be drawn the soonest! If you hate waiting, grab spots on those raffles to see the live draw within hours.

What Kinds of Pokémon Prizes Do We Pull?

Our inventory is constantly rotating. On a typical week, you will see a mix of modern sealed investments (like Sword & Shield and Scarlet & Violet era Elite Trainer Boxes), vintage holographic singles from the 1999 Base Set, and insanely valuable graded PSA 10 alternate arts. Make sure to check this page daily as new stock is added!
